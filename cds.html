About the Department

TThe Department of Computational & Data Science was established in the year 2025. The department offers B. Tech (Mathematics and Computing) and MCA (Master of Computer Applications) programs. B.Tech (Mathematics and Computing) program was started in the year 2023 by the Department of Mathematics, with an intake of 30 students. MCA program was started in the year 1986 by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering with an intake of 30 and current intake is 58.
